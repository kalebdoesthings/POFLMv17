Getaway Shootout

  • Share

  • 3

  • 5

You are going to both race and fight with the characters which move only forward and backward by bending. In this race, you may have adventage by collecting guns and some other power-ups on your way to beat your opponents. Your goal is to get to the helicopter before other players.

Game controls:
Player 1:
Hold "W" to bend backward and release it to jump to the direction where you bend.
Hold "E" to bend forward and release it to jump to the direction where you bend.
Shoot: "R"

Player 2:
Hold "I" to bend backward and release it to jump to the direction where you bend.
Hold "O" to bend forward and release it to jump to the direction where you bend.
Shoot: "P"

Have fun!
